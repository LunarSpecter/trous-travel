FAQ

Common Questions

How early should I arrive at the boarding point?+
We recommend arriving at least 15 minutes before departure. Your e-ticket QR code is your boarding pass — no need to print anything.
Can I cancel or reschedule my booking?+
Yes! Cancellations made more than 4 hours before departure receive a full refund. Rescheduling is available up to 2 hours before departure, subject to seat availability.
Is luggage allowed on board?+
Each passenger is allowed one cabin bag (up to 7 kg) and one check-in bag (up to 20 kg) in the luggage hold. Extra luggage can be added for a small fee during booking.
Are there stops for food and restrooms on long journeys?+
All journeys over 4 hours include scheduled halts at clean, well-equipped rest stops. For overnight routes, there's typically one meal halt at a highway dhaba.
How do I track my bus?+
Once your journey is confirmed, you'll receive a tracking link via SMS and email. You can also track your bus live from within the Flash Tours app available on iOS and Android.
Do you offer group booking discounts?+
Absolutely! Groups of 10 or more get up to 20% off. For charter enquiries or corporate travel programs, contact our team at groups@flashtours.in.
